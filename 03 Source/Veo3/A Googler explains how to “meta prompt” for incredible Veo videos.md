### A Googler explains how to “meta prompt” for incredible Veo videos / 一位谷歌员工解释如何通过“元提示”创作惊人的 Veo 视频

> Google DeepMind UX Engineer Anna Bortsova asks Gemini to draft detailed prompts for her videos — and the results are show-stopping.
>
> Google DeepMind 用户体验工程师 Anna Bortsova 让 Gemini 为她的视频起草详细的提示词 —— 结果令人叹为观止。

---

There’s an internal chat group where Googlers share all sorts of new AI demos. One day you might find a tool that recommends music based on a book you show it; another, an app that generates [pics of your older and younger selves](https://blog.google/products/gemini/gemini-nano-banana-examples/) hanging out.

在谷歌内部，有一个聊天群组，谷歌员工会在那里分享各种新奇的 AI 演示。某天，你可能会发现一个根据你展示的书籍推荐音乐的工具；另一天，则可能是一个能生成[你自己老年和年轻时在一起的照片](https://blog.google/products/gemini/gemini-nano-banana-examples/)的应用程序。

---

On many days, you’ll find the works of Google DeepMind UX Engineer Anna Bortsova.

在很多时候，你会看到 Google DeepMind 用户体验工程师 Anna Bortsova 的作品。

---

Anna — who has a background in engineering and visual arts — loves experimenting with our AI tools. She has shared AI-generated alphabets with each letter made of intricate embroidery, and surrealist interpretations of popular digital games.

Anna 拥有工程和视觉艺术背景，她热爱实验我们的 AI 工具。她分享过 AI 生成的字母表，其中每个字母都由精美的刺绣制成，还有对流行电子游戏的超现实主义解读。

Her background comes in handy. “I knew Salvador Dali was inspired by Flemish art, so I asked Gemini to produce surrealist images of the games in the style of Flemish artists,” Anna says. The resulting images went viral in the chat group, attracting emoji hearts and tons of positive comments.

她的背景派上了用场。“我知道萨尔瓦多·达利（Salvador Dali）受到佛兰德艺术的启发，所以我让 Gemini 以佛兰德艺术家的风格创作游戏的超现实主义图像，”Anna 说。生成的图像在聊天群中走红，吸引了大量的爱心表情包和好评。

---

More recently, Anna has used Veo to produce a series of short ASMR-style videos featuring stop-motion, paper-engineered scenes. She’s created videos of a skewer being barbecued — speared chunks of scrunched-up paper “meat” rotating above a mound of scrunched-up paper “coals” — and of a pink flamingo flapping its paper wings with a soothing flutter and whoosh.

最近，Anna 使用 Veo 制作了一系列短片，这些短片是采用定格动画和纸艺（paper-engineered）场景的 ASMR 风格视频。她制作了烧烤肉串的视频 —— 用皱巴巴的纸做的“肉块”串在签子上，在同样用皱巴巴的纸做的“煤堆”上旋转 —— 还有一只粉色火烈鸟拍打纸质翅膀，伴随着悦耳的拍击声和呼呼声。

---

“Veo 3 provides high-quality video and really strong sound,” Anna says. “There’s something so satisfying about the rustling of the paper in these videos.”

“Veo 3 提供了高质量的视频和非常强劲的音效，”Anna 说。“这些视频中纸张沙沙作响的声音让人感到非常治愈。”

---

Others seem to agree. Not only have Anna’s creations been attracting serious emoji love in the chat, but marketing teams across Google have reached out to showcase her works [on our social channels](https://x.com/GoogleAI/status/1940822370132152442). She also posts her works on her own social media, where people from outside Google have asked for her prompting advice.

其他人似乎也这么认为。Anna 的创作不仅在聊天群中获得了满满的爱心，谷歌各地的营销团队也主动联系，希望在我们的[社交渠道](https://x.com/GoogleAI/status/1940822370132152442)上展示她的作品。她还把自己的作品发在个人社交媒体上，谷歌以外的人也会向她请教提示词建议。

---

“The thing is,” Anna says, “Gemini is the one actually writing the prompts.”

“问题在于，”Anna 说，“实际上是 Gemini 在写提示词。”

---

Anna employs an approach known as “meta prompting.” Rather than writing a prompt for a specific scene, she asks Gemini to draft detailed prompts for several different scenes — sometimes 5-10 at a time — to use in [Flow](https://labs.google/flow/about) or [the Gemini app](https://gemini.google.com/). The resulting prompts can be incredibly long and specific — sometimes multiple pages — and result in breathtaking output. But the prompts she uses to instruct Gemini on how to create its prompts are key.

Anna 采用了一种被称为“元提示（meta prompting）”的方法。她不是为一个特定的场景编写提示词，而是让 Gemini 为几个不同的场景起草详细的提示词 —— 有时一次起草 5 到 10 个 —— 以用于 [Flow](https://labs.google/flow/about) 或 [Gemini 应用](https://gemini.google.com/)。生成的提示词可能非常长且具体 —— 有时长达数页 —— 并产生令人惊叹的输出。但她用来指示 Gemini 如何创建这些提示词的“指令”才是关键。

Anna’s meta prompts inspire Gemini to produce richly detailed prompts for instructing a gen AI model.

```
generate 5 prompts for an llm that will produce a short (8s) animation, which feature a paper-engineered scenes features BOTANICAL ART (flowers, plants, insects). the style should be stop motion video. Make sure the prompts capture the fact that everything is made out of paper and captured in a stop motion animation way. Try to think of the most creative, impressive and delightful prompts. Think about scenes which are satisfying to watch
```

Anna 的元提示激励 Gemini 生成极其详尽的提示词，用于指导生成式 AI 模型。

```
生成 5 条提示词，用于让一个 LLM 生成一段简短（8 秒）的动画，内容为纸艺工程场景，主题是植物艺术（花朵、植物、昆虫）。整体风格应为定格动画视频。务必在提示词中体现出所有元素都是由纸张制作而成，并且以定格动画的方式进行拍摄。尽量构思最具创意、最令人印象深刻、最令人愉悦的提示词。思考那些观看起来非常解压、令人满足的场景。
```

---

“There are no rules here — we’re experimenting — but I’ve found a few things that help steer Gemini to really rich prompts,” she says. “You want to define a very specific task: ‘write a detailed prompt that an LLM will understand.’ And you want to be clear about your format and style: say, an 8-second stop-motion animation of paper-engineered scenes. Then give it constraints, like foil paper or shiny paper, rather than just general paper. Then let it do its thing.”

“这里没有规则 —— 我们正在实验 —— 但我发现了一些可以帮助引导 Gemini 生成极其丰富提示词的方法，”她说。“你需要定义一个非常具体的任务：‘编写一段大语言模型能理解的详细提示词’。你还要明确格式和风格：比如，一段 8 秒的纸艺场景定格动画。然后给它约束条件，比如使用箔纸或亮光纸，而不仅仅是普通的纸。最后放手让它去发挥。”

---

Depending on how a model responds to Gemini’s prompts, you may want to tweak them, she says. Add or change details about the sounds and textures you want to produce — it’s a collaboration. You might also want to get emotional. “I’ve found it helps to suggest the feeling you want to evoke,” she adds. “Tell Gemini you want it to think about ‘scenes which are satisfying to watch,’ for example.”

她说，根据模型对 Gemini 提示词的反应，你可能需要对它们进行微调。增加或更改关于你想要生成的音频和纹理的细节 —— 这是一个协作的过程。你可能还需要带入情感。“我发现建议想要唤起的感觉很有帮助，”她补充道。“例如，告诉 Gemini 你希望它构思‘观看时让人感到愉悦的场景’。”

---

With such instructions and the task of creating botanical art, Gemini delivered a prompt for an unfurling paper fern in which “the animation should be slow and mesmerizing, with each frond delicately unfolding in a gentle, rhythmic sequence.” Veo understood the assignment.

在这些指令以及创作植物艺术的任务下，Gemini 提供了一个关于纸蕨徐徐展开的提示词，其中提到“动画应该是缓慢且迷人的，每一片叶子都在温柔、有节奏的序列中精致地展开。”Veo 领会了这个要求。

---

Anna’s ferns and feathers are not part of her core work: Day-to-day, she helps build the infrastructure and tools for Google DeepMind’s researchers to scale their AI experiments. But it’s something that gives her joy when she finds a spare 10 minutes, and she’s happy to share the love. (She even created a deck to pass on her learnings.)

Anna 制作的蕨类植物和羽毛并不是她的核心工作：日常生活中，她负责帮助构建基础设施和工具，以便 Google DeepMind 的研究人员扩展他们的 AI 实验。但当她有 10 分钟空闲时间时，这些创作会带给她快乐，而且她乐于分享这份热爱。（她甚至制作了一个幻灯片来传授她的心得。）

---

Her biggest tip for Googlers… and anyone else who’s listening? “Pick a subject you love and just start experimenting,” she says. “That’s what I did, and I’m still learning — and having fun.”

她对谷歌员工……以及其他听众最大的建议是什么？“选择一个你喜欢的主题，然后开始实验，”她说。“这就是我所做的，我仍然在学习 —— 并且乐在其中。”
